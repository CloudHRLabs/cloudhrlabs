## What is Workday?

Workday is a cloud-based Human Capital Management (HCM) and Financial Management platform.

Unlike traditional ERP systems, Workday was built entirely for the cloud.

---

## Why Companies Choose Workday

- Modern UI
- Cloud Native
- Highly Secure
- Continuous Updates
- Mobile Friendly
- Configurable without heavy coding
